**What if your child loved learning so much, they asked for more classes in the middle of their holiday?**

Leonardo, a curious and consistent math student, had been loving his math classes at Strive. Just before a planned one-month summer holiday, his parent reached out: "Can we try a coding class before we leave? Just to see how it goes."

The plan was simple: one class, then pause everything for the break.

But just 15–20 minutes after that first coding class ended, the parent got back in touch: "Leonardo doesn't want to wait. He wants to keep going, right away!"

Why? Because in just one trial class, his teacher Monisha didn't just introduce coding — she made it click. She tuned into how Leonardo learns, wove in his interests, and made him feel safe to explore and enjoy the process.

By the end of the session, Leonardo wasn't just curious — he was hooked. It was the mix of a teacher who really saw him and a class that made coding feel doable and genuinely fun.

This is what we love to see at Strive: students so engaged, they choose learning even on their holidays.
